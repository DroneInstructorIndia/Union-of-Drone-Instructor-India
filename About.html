About Us

The Union of Drone Instructor India is a free and open platform designed for all Indian drone instructors and pilots. Our mission is to create a space where DGCA-certified instructors and drone operators can come together, share knowledge, and stay updated on the latest advancements in drone technology in India.

This website serves as a knowledge hub, providing free access to important updates, regulatory changes, technical resources, and opportunities for continuous learning. Whether you're just starting your drone journey or are an experienced professional, we believe in the power of community to uplift and advance the drone industry in India.

Some of the key benefits we offer include:

  • Access to the latest drone regulations and DTC (Drone Training Circular) updates.
  • Guidance materials and training manuals to help instructors improve their skills.
  • A community-driven approach to share experiences, tips, and best practices.
  • Regular updates on government policies, certifications, and drone pilot licensing.
  • Free resources, including PDFs, instructional videos, and guides.

Disclaimer: This is not a government website. The platform is intended to help users stay updated with Indian drone technology and explore drone-related knowledge through the contribution of expertise and information from the community.
